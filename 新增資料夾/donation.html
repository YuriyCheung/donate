About Me

Hello! I'm Yuriy Cheung, currently studying for a Higher Diploma in Data Science at HKBU-SCE. I'm passionate about computer science and technology because they're not just cool - they actually make people's lives better!

My journey began in Primary 6 with the 'Hour of Code' program, where I discovered that telling computers what to do is way more fun than being told what to do by humans. Since then, I've learned Python, HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript, and even some data analytics models that can predict things (like how many cups of coffee I'll need to survive finals).

I've worked as an IT student helper at HKBU and even taught kids Python (they're surprisingly good at finding bugs in my code). I've also traveled to Singapore to learn about AI that can generate music - because why should humans have all the fun?

When I'm not coding, I play guitar, paint, and build amazing things in Minecraft using command coding. I've created real-life useful tools too, like currency converters and class schedulers for my classmates.

My ultimate goal? To use technology to solve real problems and make the world a better place. But first, I need to get through university!
